HTML Tags I Have Learned

p
Defines a paragraph of text.
h1-h6
Defines headings, from most important (h1) to least important (h6).
img
Displays an image on the webpage.
a
Creates a hyperlink to another page or website.
ul and li
Creates an unordered list and list items.
